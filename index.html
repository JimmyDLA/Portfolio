About Me

Hi my name is Jimmy De Los Angeles I was born in New York, New York, raised in the Bronx until the age of 5 before moving to the Dominican Republic with my family for 11 years. I came back to the United States to finish high school and attend college. I'm grateful for this experience because it has been extremely humbling. Initially, I was guided by the adults in my life to pursue a career in finance and accounting but I quickly realized that was not my calling. I had always been a very curious individual who always wanted to know how everything works. Coding is the manifestation of my curiousity. Imagination has no boundaries and the possibilities are virtually endless. This is the greatest aspect of a career that I am excited to be apart of.
